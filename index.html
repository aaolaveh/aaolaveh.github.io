Welcome to my personal website!

I’m a doctoral student of mathematics at Michigan State University (MSU). My advisor is Professor Elizabeth Munch and we are currently working on interleaving distances for posets.

Prior to MSU, I completed my MSc in Applied Mathematics in La Universidad Nacional de Colombia in 2021. I completed my B.Sc in Mathematics at the same university (with honors) in 2018.

Currently I am working bounding interleaving distances on concrete categories using a loss function and a family of interleaving distances defined on partial ordered sets.

More information can be found in my CV .

Research interests:

  • Algebraic topology
  • Topological data analysis: Foundations and applications
  • Mathematical biology
