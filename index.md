Welcome to my personal website!

I'm a doctoral student of mathematics at <a href="https://math.msu.edu/" target=_blank > Michigan State University </a> (MSU) under the great supervision of <a href="http://elizabethmunch.com/" target=_blank > Elizabeth Munch </a>. 

My research interests are the foundations and applications of topological data analysis. Currently, I am working on various problems on interleaving distances, particularly on the generalization and computation of such distance for modules defined over arbitrary posets.

Beyond my research, I am thriving as a mathematics instructor at MSU and finding great joy in teaching. You can discover more about my teaching experiences and philosophy in my  <a href="/Portfolio/teaching_portfolio/" target=_self> teaching portfolio </a>.

Prior to MSU, I completed my MSc in Applied Mathematics in <a href="https://unal.edu.co/" target=_blank > La Universidad Nacional de Colombia </a> in 2021. I completed my B.Sc in Mathematics at the same university (with honors) in 2018.
